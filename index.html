About Us

The Brasília International Church has the main goal of assisting to the need of the large immigrant community in the city – to have a place of worship to God and preaching the gospel in English and Spanish.

Vision

A relevant church to the international community, reaching out, embracing and restoring in Christ.

We Believe

As part of the Seventh-Day Adventist church, the BIC considers all the Holy Bible to be trustworthy and only rule of faith and hope. Its doctrines, therefore, fully follow the bible teachings and are substantiated on them.

Mission

Be an intercultural Seventh-Day Adventist church in Brasilia that makes disciples of all nations.
